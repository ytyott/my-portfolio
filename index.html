About Me

Hello! I'm Rajesh, a photographer by passion. My journey with photography began years ago, and I've been captivated by the art of capturing moments ever since.

I'm still learning and exploring new techniques every day. My goal is to tell a story with every shot, focusing on light, emotion, and the tiny details that make each moment unique.

Whether it's landscapes, portraits, or street photography, I'm always searching for a new perspective.
